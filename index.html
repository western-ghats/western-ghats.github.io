Cooking Tips

1 Sachet = 1 Perfect Curry

🔸 How to Use
  1. 1 Use 1 full sachet (10–12g) for 1 curry (serves 3–5 people)
  2. 2 Add at the final stage of cooking (last 3–5 minutes)
  3. 3 Mix well and cook on low flame for best aroma
🔸 Perfect For
🍗 Chicken Curry
🥩 Beef Curry
🥕 Vegetable Curry
🥚 Egg Curry
🧀 Paneer Dishes
⭐ Pro Cooking Tips
  • ✅ Always add garam masala after gravy thickens
  • ✅ Do not overcook after adding – it reduces aroma
  • ✅ Add a little ghee or coconut oil after adding masala for enhanced flavor
  • ✅ Close lid for 2 minutes after adding for aroma locking
📊 Quantity Guide
Amount Use Case
1 Sachet Perfect for 500g meat / 400g vegetables
¾ Sachet For mild taste
Full Sachet For strong aroma
🌿 Best results with fresh ingredients & low flame cooking 🌿
