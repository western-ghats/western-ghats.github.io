Cooking Tips

1 Sachet = 1 Perfect Curry

Simple steps for the best aroma every time ✨

🔸 How to Use
  1. 1Use 1 full sachet (10–12g) for 1 curry – serves 3–5 people
  2. 2Add at the final stage of cooking (last 3–5 minutes)
  3. 3Mix well and cook on low flame for best aroma
🔸 Perfect For
🍗Chicken Curry
🥩Beef Curry
🥕Vegetable Curry
🥚Egg Curry
🧀Paneer Dishes
⭐ Pro Cooking Tips
  • ✅Always add garam masala after gravy thickens
  • ✅Do not overcook after adding – it reduces aroma
  • ✅Add ghee or coconut oil after masala for enhanced flavor
  • ✅Close lid for 2 minutes after adding for aroma locking

AmountUse Case
